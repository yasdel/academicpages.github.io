            <br>
            <p>I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of <a class="style26" href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/"> Computer Science</a> @ <a class="style26" href="umd.edu/"> University of Maryland </a> working with <a class="style26" href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/~tomg/"> Prof. Tom Goldstein</a>. I hold a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering/Operations Research from University of Maryland under the supervision of <a class="style26" href="https://cee.umd.edu/clark/faculty/269/Ali-Haghani"> Prof. Ali Haghani</a>. Prior to that, I received my bachelor's degree from <a class="style26" href="http://www.sharif.ir/web/en/"> Sharif University of Technology</a>. My research focuses on using optimization to solve important problems in machine learning and operations research. Recently, I have been studying adversarial machine learning with the goal of making AI systems more secure, robust, and interpretable. My work has applications in Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Secuirty. Due to my background in Operations Research, I am also interested in developing algorithms and models that can be used to improve the operation of large systems. My research has an emphasis on solving real world problems with impacts in business, and has received both academic and <a href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/~ashafahi/media.pdf">media </a> recognition. You can find my CV <a href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/~ashafahi/AShafahiCV.pdf">here</a>.</p>
