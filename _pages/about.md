Since January 2018, I am a research assistant at Information Retrieval Laboratory of University of Milano Bicocca, Italy working with <a href="http://www.ir.disco.unimib.it/people/pasi-gabriella/">Prof. Gabriella Pasi</a>. I obtained my PhD (with distinction) from Politecnico di Milano under the lead of <a href="http://www.deib.polimi.it/eng/people/details/159156">Paolo Cremonesi</a>. My research interests include recommender systems in particular multimedia recommender systems (videos, music or images) with focous multimedia computing from one hand and user modeling/personalization from the other side. During my PhD, I was an exchange student at Johannes Kepler University Linz, Austria for six month and had the pleasure to work with <a href="http://www.cp.jku.at/people/schedl/">Markus Schedl</a>.  I have been involved in organizing ACM RecSys Challenges 2017 (hosted by <a href="https://www.xing.com/en">Xing</a>), 2018 (hosted by <a href="https://www.spotify.com/it/">Spotify</a>) and the upcoming 2019 challenge (to be hosted by <a href="https://www.trivago.com/">Trivago</a>) all being part of the <a href="https://recsys.acm.org">ACM Conference on Recommender Systems</a>. I am also leading organization of a task at MediaEval Benchmarking Initiative for Multimedia Evaluation (MediaEval 2018) named: "The 2018 Recommending Movies Using Content: Which content is key?".
